New Project at Cubert, Newquay

— 6 Sep 2022

Gwinear is a new-build two-storey dwelling currently under construction at Cubert, near Newquay.

This traditionally built detached home will also benefit from an adjacent single-storey triple garage and gym building. The design makes extensive use of granite in the elevations, quoins, lintols and cills.

The professionals working with the Chapman team on this one include Cornish based CSA Architects, and engineers Ballantine Arnold of Kingsbridge in Devon.
