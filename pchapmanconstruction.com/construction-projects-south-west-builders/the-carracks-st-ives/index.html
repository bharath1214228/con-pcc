The Carracks, St Ives

This project consisted of twenty two new-build family houses for renowned developers Acorn Blue.

The properties, on the Zennor Road as you leave St Ives, were constructed on the site of a former night club which was demolished. They all have views of Rosewall Hill, and the project makes good use of natural seedum roof coverings.

Architectural work was by A-Tec Design and the engineers for the project were MBA Consulting.

P Chapman Construction was very pleased to work with regular clients Acorn on this successful project.
