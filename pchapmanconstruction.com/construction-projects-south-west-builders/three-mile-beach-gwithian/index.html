Three Mile Beach, Gwithian

Three Mile Beach is at Gwithian, near St Ives in Cornwall, and comprises fifteen large beach houses which are currently offered for holiday let.

The units are timber-framed, and the Chapman site team worked to complete the project with local professionals, including A-Tec Design, Ed Crossley & Associates (QS) and MBA Consulting (engineers).

The development is located right behind the dunes in Gwithian. The Three Mile Beach website contains more details of this exciting project.
