Trelowen, St Agnes

Set high on the cliffs above St Agnes, with stunning sea views, Trelowen is one in a long line of very special luxury dwellings from P Chapman Construction Ltd.

The Chapman Team worked with regular partners on this one, the thoughtful design being by CSA Architects, and the extensive structural details including retaining structures were courtesy of MBA Consulting; both practices are locally based in Truro.

Natural stonework helps ensure this very fine home fits well into its stunning Cornish surroundings.
