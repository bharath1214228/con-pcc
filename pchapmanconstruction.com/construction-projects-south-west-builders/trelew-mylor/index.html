Trelew, Mylor

Work to waterside and luxury residences is a particular strength of P Chapman Construction Ltd. This project is a fine example of this proven capability.

Trelew, at Mylor is a lovely detached house which benefited from a complete highly-bespoke interior refurbishment as well as an extension which was tastefully finished in vertical slate.

The professional team for the project included CSA Architects, MBA (Consulting Engineers) and Absolute Interior Design.
