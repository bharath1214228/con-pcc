Why choose us as builders?

Building projects all present their own individual challenges – no two are ever the same.

We can build for you anywhere in Cornwall. Our experience covers all aspects of construction: New Build, Conversions, Extension, Refurbishment and Development. 

We have a considerable expertise in Eco-buildings and are able to work on all forms of contract including Traditional and Design & Build. 

We have a great team of multi-skilled people available to work on your project and we aim to exceed all expectations in every aspect of the build.

We will oversee your project from conception to completion and look to communicate openly and collaboratively, making sure your needs are met and the project delivered successfully.

Please have a browse through our Projects section where you will see a large portfolio of very happy clients in both the Residential and Commercial sectors.

P Chapman Construction are well known for building unique beautiful luxury homes as well as renovating and extending existing properties, including extensive knowledge of eco-builds and barns conversions.  Our passion for creating and developing beautiful homes and our attention to detail, allows us to build your dream home.

Our extensive knowledge of building in the commercial sector, including offices, schools and shops, allow us to have a diverse portfolio.  Our highly qualified tradespeople are able to work on varied complex projects and always deliver to an extremely high standard.

We work with private clients and developers alike and have excellent working relationships with architects, engineers, specialist designers and many other services.  We have a great rapport with a wide range of reliable and reputable local suppliers, along with links to a broad spectrum of specialist merchants.

We have a wealth of knowledge and experience to provide original and proficient solutions to all your building challenges and will deliver your project cost effectively and to the highest standards.
